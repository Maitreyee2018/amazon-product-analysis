# amazon-product-analysis
Exploratory analysis of Amazon product dataset covering pricing, ratings, discounts and customer reviews.

Amazon Sales Dataset is having the data of 1K+ Amazon Product's Ratings and Reviews as per their details listed on the official website of Amazon.
It is having the following features - 
                                    product_id - Product ID
                                    product_name - Name of the Product
                                    category - Category of the Product
                                    discounted_price - Discounted Price of the Product
                                    actual_price - Actual Price of the Product
                                    discount_percentage - Percentage of Discount for the Product
                                    rating - Rating of the Product
                                    rating_count - Number of people who voted for the Amazon rating
                                    about_product - Description about the Product
                                    user_id - ID of the user who wrote review for the Product
                                    user_name - Name of the user who wrote review for the Product
                                    review_id - ID of the user review
                                    review_title - Short review
                                    review_content - Long review
                                    img_link - Image Link of the Product
                                    product_link - Official Website Link of the Product

Project Overview -
  This project explores an e-commerce product dataset to understand how pricing, discounts, and customer feedback influence, product performance. 
  The analysis focuses on identifying relationships between discounted_price, discount_percentage, ratings and review behaviour, while also examining customer review text to uncover common themes behind positivie and negative experiences. 
  By combining quantitative analysis with basic text processing, the prject aims to highlight patterns that can support product positioning, value discovery and recommendation strategies in online retail platform.

Key Questions -
 Does pricing affect ratings?
 Do discounts influence satisfaction?
 What customers say in review?

Key Insights -
  Ratings are relatively stable across price ranges.
  Discounts alone do not improve customer satisfaction.
  Customer reviews are influenced by product experience and not by pricing tactics.
